How to trust this. The official jobs data (JOLTS) measures how many jobs end, but never why. “Caused by AI” is a claim — so we tag every layoff on two separate questions (did it happen · is it AI) and never blur them. Coverage skews to tech and large firms. We surface claims; we do not certify that AI caused any specific job loss.
